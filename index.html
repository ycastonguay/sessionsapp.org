Sessions
 is a music player designed to help musicians practice their instruments.

It is available for free on desktop, tablet and smartphones (iOS, Android, Mac, Linux and Windows).

Download Sessions

Loops and Markers allow you to practice any part of a song, making your practice session much more efficient. That way, you can keep your hands on your instruments.

Time Shifting makes it easier to pick up complex rythmic sections or practice a song at a slower or higher tempo. You can slow down or speed up audio from 50% up to 150%.
