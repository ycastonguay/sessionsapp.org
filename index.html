Sessions
 is a music player designed to help musicians practice their instruments.

It is available for free on desktop, tablet and smartphones (iOS, Android, Mac, Linux and Windows).

Download Sessions

Loops & Markers

Allows you to practice any part of a song and keep your hands on your instrument, making your practice session much more efficient.

Time Shifting

Makes it easier to pick up complex rythmic sections or practice a song at a slower or higher tempo (50% to 150%).
